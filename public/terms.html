Only the resulting text transcript is stored. • Camera & Photo Library (optional): Required only if you choose to attach a photo in Arya's chat. No data is transmitted to our servers during this background process. Compass push notifications ("a letter from Arya") are delivered via the Expo Push Service to APNs (iOS) / FCM (Android) — see Privacy Policy §8. All permissions are requested at the point of use and can be revoked at any time from your device Settings. Revoking a permission disables the corresponding feature but does not affect other App functionality.
